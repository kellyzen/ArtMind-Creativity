ArtMind: Art Analysis Tool for Creativity

by Kelly Tan | 16 December 2023

A journey through imagination...

You know how being creative is like having a superpower? It helps you come up with awesome ideas, solve problems in cool ways, and express yourself uniquely. But sometimes, it's hard to know just how creative we really are, especially when we're online, right?

That's where ArtMind swoops in to save the day! It's a fun website where you can see how creative you are by drawing stuff by leverage the power of AI. And it's not just about drawing – you'll learn some really cool stuff about creativity test too, like how the creativity scores are calculated.

When you draw stuffs on ArtMind, it's not just for fun - it's like taking a creativity test! It's kind of like those tests you take in school, but way more fun. ArtMind uses the Torrance Test of Creative Thinking-Figural (TCTT-F), specifically the Repeated Figures Test to check out your drawings. It's like a creativity scorecard! And don't worry if you don't get it all at first – ArtMind explains everything in simple terms so you can understand how creative you really are.

So, hop on ArtMind, unleash your creativity, and let's see just how awesome you really are!
