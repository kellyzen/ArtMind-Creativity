What is Torrance Test of Creativity Thinking- Figural (TTCT-F)?

TTCT-F is a widely used assessment tool that measures an individual's creative thinking abilities through tasks involving drawing and visual expression. It evaluates various aspects of creativity, including fluency, originality, elaboration, and abstractness of titles.

In ArtMind, one activity that we aim to incorporate from the TTCT-F is the Repeated Figures activity. This task involves participants working with series of figures that are repeated (straight lines or circles). By engaging in this exercise, participants are encouraged to explore different creative possibilities within a structured framework. This activity offers valuable insights into participants' creative thinking processes, including their ability to iterate on existing ideas, adapt to variations, and generate novel interpretations.
