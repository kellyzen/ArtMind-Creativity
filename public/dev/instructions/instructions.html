What is Torrance Test of Creativity Thinking- Figural (TTCT-F)?

TTCT-F is a widely used assessment tool that measures an individual's creative thinking abilities through tasks involving drawing and visual expression. It evaluates various aspects of creativity, including fluency, originality, elaboration, and abstractness of titles.

In ArtMind's assessment methodology, one activity that we aim to incorporate from the TTCT-F is the Repeated Figures activity. This task involves participants working with series of figures that are repeated or slightly varied. By engaging in this exercise, participants are encouraged to explore different creative possibilities within a structured framework. The Repeated Figures activity offers valuable insights into participants' creative thinking processes, including their ability to iterate on existing ideas, adapt to variations, and generate novel interpretations.

How to use ArtMind?

  1. Review and check the terms and conditions.
  2. Navigate to the Test tab.
  3. Upon clicking the "let's start" button, a 5-minute timer will start.
  4. Complete all 18 figures within the provided bounding box.
  5. Feel free to choose colours and expand the canvas as needed.
  6. You have the option to quit or submit the test in the mean time.
  7. Once the 5-minute countdown, the test will end. The evaluation will take 3-10 seconds and automatically redirects you to the result page.

Controls:

  • Drawing: Left-click or single-finger touch
  • Moving: Right-click or two-finger touch
  • Zoom in/out: Scroll wheel or pinch
